Returns & Refunds Policy

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. If you need to make a return or cancel an order, please read our policy below to understand the process.

Cancellation Policy

We understand that sometimes plans change. If you wish to cancel your order, please inform us at least 1 day before the scheduled delivery time.

To cancel your order, please contact us directly via the Contact Us page. Our team will assist you with the cancellation process.

If the delivery man has already been dispatched and is on their way, cancellations will not be accepted. However, you can still refuse the order at the time of delivery, but in such cases:

  • You will be charged the delivery fee and
  • You will be required to pay half of the total order amount as a cancellation fee.

Returns and Exchanges

Unfortunately, due to the perishable nature of our products, we cannot accept returns or exchanges after the vegetables have been delivered. Please inspect your order upon arrival to ensure everything is correct.

If you receive damaged or incorrect items, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery. We will assess the issue and offer a suitable resolution, such as a replacement or refund, depending on the situation.

Refund Policy

If you are eligible for a refund due to an issue with your order (e.g., incorrect or damaged items), the refund will be processed through your original payment method. Please note the following:

  • Refunds will be processed within 7 business days after approval.
  • If a partial order is canceled, the refund will be based on the canceled items, excluding the delivery fee.
  • In cases where cancellation occurs after the delivery man has left the warehouse but before the delivery is made, you will still be charged the delivery fee and a half payment cancellation fee.
